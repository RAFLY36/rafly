About Us

We are students from the College of Computer Science, and Rafly is a project we built out of a simple but powerful idea: Why let university books collect dust when they can help someone else?

Rafly is a platform that allows students to share, sell, or give away their textbooks to others who need them.

Our goal is to support education, reduce waste, and promote sustainability.

This project aligns with Saudi Arabia‚Äôs Vision 2030 by encouraging smart resource use, empowering youth, and enhancing access to educational materials.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Rafly?

Rafly is an online platform that allows university students to exchange, buy, or give away textbooks they no longer need, making academic resources more accessible to everyone.

2. Do I need to create an account to use Rafly?

No, you can start browsing and searching for books right away without creating an account.

3. How can I find books I need?

You can search for books by title, author, subject, or condition. Rafly helps you easily find textbooks that match your needs and budget.

4. Is Rafly free to use?

Yes! You can browse books, list your books, and connect with other students at no cost.

5. How do I contact a seller?

You can directly contact the seller using the contact information they provide.

6. Can I give away my books for free?

Absolutely! Rafly gives you the option to offer your books for free.

7. How is Rafly related to Vision 2030?

Rafly supports Vision 2030‚Äôs goals by promoting sustainability, reducing waste, and enhancing education.
